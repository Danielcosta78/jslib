Why Use CDN Libraries?

Faster Loading

Files are served from geographically distributed servers, reducing latency.

Caching Advantage

Users may already have cached versions from other sites.

Reduced Bandwidth

Offload static assets from your own servers.

Security

Reputable CDNs provide DDoS protection and integrity checks.
